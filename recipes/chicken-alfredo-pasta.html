Chicken Alfredo Pasta

This chicken alfredo pasta recipe is creamy, flavorful, and perfect for a comforting meal!

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Chicken breast
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

How to make Chicken Alfredo Pasta Step by Step

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta al dente.
  2. Cook the chicken breast until golden brown.
  3. In a separate pan, melt butter and saut√© garlic.
  4. Add heavy cream and simmer until slightly thickened.
  5. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  6. Add cooked pasta and chicken to the sauce, toss to coat.
  7. Season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.

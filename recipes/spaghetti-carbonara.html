Spaghetti Carbonara

This spaghetti carbonara recipe is creamy, delicious, and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta or bacon
  • Eggs
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Black pepper

How to make Spaghetti Carbonara Step by Step

  1. Cook the spaghetti al dente.
  2. Cook the pancetta or bacon until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and toss with the egg mixture.
  5. Add the cooked pancetta or bacon and mix well.
  6. Serve immediately.
